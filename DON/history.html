Founded in 1956



Delta Omega Nu was created by a group 16 men with simular interest in Memorial Hall at Carthage College, Carthage Illinois. The fraternity started out as a group of athletes seeking to play during off seasons, but quickly became very popular. These men first called themselves the noodles. Soon after forming, they went to the head of the college to be reconized as a social fraternity.
